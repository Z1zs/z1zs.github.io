This research line focuses on trustworthy large language and multimodal models, including watermarking, unlearning, and interpretability.

Selected directions:

- Designed LLM watermarking methods with distortion-free generation and robustness guarantees under semantic-level attacks.
- Developed multimodal unlearning methods for MLLMs that erase target visual knowledge while preserving unrelated capabilities.
- Studied cross-modality mechanisms in multimodal models through neuron-level and knowledge-circuit analysis.

Related papers include PMark, SAMark, MMUnlearner, MMNeuron, and the knowledge-circuit analysis work listed on the publications page.
