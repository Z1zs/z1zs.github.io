CausalEmbed studies auto-regressive multi-vector generation in latent space for visual-document embedding. The project targets representation learning for documents that require joint reasoning over visual layout, text, and semantic structure.

Research focus:

- Developed and optimized auto-regressive multimodal embedding models for visual-document representation learning.
- Compared training dynamics between generative multimodal models and representation-oriented multimodal models.
- Explored reasoning-based methods for multimodal representation learning.

This line of work is listed in the CV as under review.
